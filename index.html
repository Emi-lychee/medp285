What is Genshin Impact?

Genshin Impact is a role-playing game that was developed and produced by HoYoverse. It was released on September 28, 2020 on Android, iOS, Windows, and PlayStation 4.

The world of Genshin Impact takes place in a fantasy world called Teyvat, with seven nations bound to seven elements and each ruled by a different Archon. As of right now, only three of the nations have been released for players to explore: Mondstadt, the city of freedom; Liyue, the city of contracts; and Inazuma, the city of eternity.

About HoYoverse

HoYoverse, previously known as miHoYo, is an interactive entertainment company. They created many products, such as games (Genshin Impact, Honkai Impact 3rd, Tears of Themis, and Honkai: Star Rail), desktop software (N0va Desktop), communities (HoYoLAB), animations, comics, music, novels, and merchandise.



Genshin Impact Community

Following the release of Genshin Impact, players from around the world came together to connect and form new communities across different social media platforms, such as Discord, Facebook, Instagram, Reddit, Twitch, and more.

HoYoverse also created HoYoLAB, which is a website and app where players can share Genshin Impact related posts with a large community of members from all over the world. Recently, HoYoLAB has also expended to include the communities of other games created by HoYoverse.
