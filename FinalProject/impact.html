There is a Facebook group called Genshin Impact Official Fan Group. Here, players can post fanart, memes, cosplays, and character builds. Players can also ask for advice from other players and some even gain new friends.

This is a Discord server called Genshin Impact Tavern. In addition to sharing and discussing Genshin Impact, players can stream themselves playing for other players to see in real time.

There is another Discord server called Genshin Impact Official. It was the first official Genshin Impact community on Discord and this server quickly became full (500,000 users maximum).
